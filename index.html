About Me

Hello, I'm Nguyen (Win) from Sacramento, California. I am an aspiring Full-Stack Web Developer currently taking the Full-Stack Web Developer Nanodegree at Udacity. When I was younger, I expressed my creativity and thoughts through drawing. Throughout high school, I taught myself how to use Adobe Photoshop to create layouts, backgrounds, images, flyers, and other graphic works while learning a bit of HTML and CSS along the way. I am the type of person who enjoys creating and building something because to me, it feels rewarding.

While I was working full-time at my last job, I came to a realization that that job was not for me. I was introduced to the idea of coding from friends and family and eventually, I resigned from that job to pursue a career in web development. Since then, I have been learning and teaching myself computer programming languages with the help of online resources. It's a long time coming but my goal is to become a Full-Stack Web developer. As a developer, I want to express my way of thinking and creativity through coding and to build amazing web and mobile applications. It would be an incredible journey and experience.
